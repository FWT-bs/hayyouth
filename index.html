Our Mission

Harmony Association for Youth (HAY) is a WA registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization designated to provide an avenue for more opportunities and activities to youths to aid their development of talent and skill in areas including performance, art, and academics, with the intention of fostering a community which encourages youths to take initiative in pursuing such endeavors.

How Our Youth Serve Community

We encourage youth to use their own skills and strengths to serve and dedicate to the community, fostering in them a sense of social responsibility, honing their leadership talents, and helping them become outstanding citizens.

Social Responsibility

Building awareness and commitment to community needs

Leadership Development

Honing leadership talents through hands-on experience

Outstanding Citizens

Helping youth become positive contributors to society

What We Offer

Comprehensive programs designed to empower and support youth development

Music & Vocal Performance

Develop musical talents through voice training, instrument lessons, and ensemble performances that showcase youth abilities.

Theater & Drama

Build confidence and expression through acting, stage performance, and theatrical productions.

Visual Arts

Explore creativity through painting, drawing, sculpture, and digital art to develop artistic skills and personal expression.

Media Arts

Learn photography, video production, and multimedia creation to tell stories and document community impact.

Academic Excellence

Comprehensive tutoring, study skills development, and educational support to help youth achieve their academic goals.

Critical Thinking

Develop analytical and problem-solving skills through engaging activities and educational challenges.

Youth Stage

A dynamic platform where youth showcase their talents through performances, theatrical productions, and creative presentations that inspire and engage the community.

Public Lecture

Educational and inspirational speaking events where youth and community leaders share knowledge, experiences, and insights on important topics.

Leadership Training

Workshops and programs designed to develop essential leadership skills and foster initiative-taking among youth.

Community Service

Opportunities for youth to use their skills and strengths to serve the community and develop social responsibility.
