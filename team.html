Carolyn Xu

Team Leader

I'm Carolyn Xu, and I have been an active member of HAY since its inception, contributing significantly to its development and growth. I am passionate about music and enjoy playing both the piano and violin, as well as exploring photography and creating vibrant handmade crafts. I organize youth music events with the goal of giving back to the community and fostering meaningful connections through music. I also help manage and update HAY's YouTube channel, using my creativity to produce and edit videos that capture and preserve special moments. I'm proud to be part of building HAY and organizing events, and I remain committed to helping the organization grow and strengthen our community.

Tristan Ye

Web Team Manager

I'm Tristan Ye, a student at Inglemoor High School in Kenmore, Washington. Outside of the classroom, you'll often find me on the soccer field, engrossed in a game of chess, strumming my guitar, coding, or diving into video games. One unique aspect of my life is my role as a volunteer at HAY, a local non-profit organization. I contribute by helping to build and maintain the organization's website. Soccer has been a constant in my life, teaching me teamwork, dedication, and resilience—qualities that have proven invaluable in my role at HAY, where collaboration and commitment are essential. Chess is another hobby I'm passionate about. It sharpens my strategic thinking and helps me stay composed under pressure. My love for playing the guitar allows me to connect with others through music, which has become a meaningful source of personal fulfillment. Coding and video games are also important to me, providing both a creative outlet and opportunities to explore problem-solving in the digital world. Through my volunteer work, I've been able to blend these diverse interests and skills to support the development and operation of HAY's website—a vital part of our community outreach efforts.
